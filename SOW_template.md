## 2. Scope of Services

### 2.1 AI Voice (Inbound Call Handling)
- Inbound phone number provisioned via Bland.ai
- AI answers calls 24/7, collects: customer name, phone, service needed, preferred date/time, address
- Transfers to live agent if customer requests (optional)
- Sends booking confirmation to customer via SMS

### 2.2 AI Chat Widget
- Embedded chat widget for client website
- Answers service FAQs, collects booking requests
- Branded to client's business name and services
- Embed code: one `<script>` tag

### 2.3 Appointment Scheduling
- Real-time conflict detection
- Booking stored in 512AI system
- Synced to client CRM: **[CRM NAME, e.g., LawnPro]** via **[Zapier webhook / native API]**

### 2.4 Notifications
- Business owner: SMS + email per new booking with full customer and service details
- Customer: SMS confirmation within 30 seconds of booking
